# Fresh Google Ads Campaign Pack

## Files

- `WM-fresh-campaign-2026-03-15.csv`
- `WM-fresh-campaign-negatives-2026-03-15.csv`

## What this pack creates

- New paused search campaign: `WM | Search | Leads v2`
- Local service-area targeting for:
  - Wellington
  - Lower Hutt
  - Upper Hutt
  - Porirua
  - Petone
  - Kapiti Coast
- Six ad groups:
  - Window Repairs
  - Sliding Door Repairs
  - Window Stays And Hinges
  - Leaks And Reseals
  - Handles Locks And Hardware
  - Lock Picking And Lockouts
- Exact and phrase match only
- One responsive search ad per ad group
- Campaign-level negatives based on the wasted terms in your reports

## Upload order

1. Google Ads -> Tools -> Bulk actions -> Uploads
2. Upload `WM-fresh-campaign-2026-03-15.csv`
3. Preview and apply
4. Upload `WM-fresh-campaign-negatives-2026-03-15.csv`
5. Preview and apply

## After upload

1. Pause the current campaign `Window Repairs`
2. Enable the new campaign `WM | Search | Leads v2`
3. Open the new campaign settings and confirm:
   - Search partners = off
   - Final URL expansion / automatic landing pages = off
   - Location = presence in your targeted areas, not all NZ

## Why this is different

- Homepage is no longer the default destination
- Each ad group has its own landing page
- Broad match is removed
- Competitor and replacement searches are blocked at campaign level
- Weak niche groups are folded into stronger intent buckets
